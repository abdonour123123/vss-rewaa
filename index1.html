Inventory Management

Track inventory, sales, finances, and selling operations seamlessly both offline and online. Our up-to-date reports, classified by period and branches, offer comprehensive insights, including tax declaration reports.

Point-of-Sale (Cashier)

Rewaa platform offers a hassle-free point-of-sale system, simplifying sales processes and operations even offline. It empowers businesses to manage employees’ permissions based on their roles. Rewaa also supports the creation of an unlimited number of registers and issuance of invoices in compliance with Zakat, Tax, and Customs Authority regulations.

Accounting Software

As the ultimate technical partner for all businesses, Rewaa developed the Accounting Software. This system simplifies the accurate calculation of sales, expenses, and purchases throughout the business. Whether completing a sale or issuing an invoice, the data seamlessly integrates with the Accounting Software, eliminating the need for manual entry.

Rewaa App

Rewaa developed a practical App for business owners. This app provides a convenient tool to oversee and track the business and sales details, organized by product, category, and branch. Whether at the office or on the go, you can easily monitor stock and inventory levels for each product, ensuring smooth business operation.

Seamless Payment Integration

You can easily connect your payment device with Rewaa Point-of-Sale system. This ensures a smooth transfer of invoices directly from the payment device to the system, eliminating the need for manual entry and reducing the risk of human errors.
